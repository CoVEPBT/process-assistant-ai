PDF parser fixes from Training Plans test: items #2 + #4 + banner filter

Three Nintex-PDF parser improvements from the Training Plans
(Apprentices & Trainees) test. Items #1 (NOTE truncation) and #3
(activity 5 task b merging) still pending - deeper investigation
needed.

#2 - "Employer of Apprentice/Trainee" not recognised as role
   isRoleLine() only matched CoVE/RMIT prefixes, en/em dashes, or
   all-caps + role keyword. Now also recognises:
     - "Employer of <X>" / "Student of <X>" (org noun + "of"),
       with a guard against trailing "(X)" so document titles
       like "Student Training Plans (Apprentices & Trainees)"
       don't accidentally match.
     - Short noun phrase containing a role keyword ("Hiring
       Manager", "Accreditation Manager") - <= 6 words.
   Critically we do NOT match "starts with org noun" alone -
   that overshoots into document titles. 9/9 unit tests pass.

#4 - bare task letter on its own line
   PDF column extraction sometimes splits a task letter ("a") onto
   its own line, separate from its text. Pre-process pass at the
   top of parseProcedureBlock now merges any bare-letter line with
   the following line when the next line doesn't already look like
   a structural marker.

BANNER FILTER - title repeats between pages polluting task text
   Some Nintex exports repeat a short "title banner" line between
   pages (in this PDF: "Student Training Plans (Apprentices &
   Trainees)"). It was getting appended to whichever task / NOTE
   was open when it appeared in the line stream.

   New repeatedBannerLines detector in parseNintexPdf:
     - Counts how many times each short line (<=80 chars) appears,
       excluding structural markers AND roles (the same role
       appears in both the swimlane labels AND every activity that
       uses it - we don't want to filter roles).
     - Any non-structural, non-role line appearing 2+ times is
       treated as a page banner and dropped from the filtered
       line list before parseProcedureBlock sees it.
