Tracker #34: stop AI dropping conditional triggers + trailing requirements

Examples Natalie called out from the 29 May Training Plans
re-test (post commits a3940de + 26c65e9 + 70271ee):

- Activity 6.2 source: "Where a Training Agreement Variation
  is required to student nominal completion date, a training
  plan must be downloaded and amended..." - AI dropped the
  leading "Where X..." trigger clause, leaving the task with
  no idea when it fires.

Pattern: AI treats leading "Where / When / If X..." conditional
clauses and trailing requirement sentences chained after a
period as filler when tightening to 18 words. They are NOT
filler - the first is the trigger, the second is a follow-on
requirement.
